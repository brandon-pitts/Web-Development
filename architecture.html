The influence on architecture can be seen all around the world. From Paris to Japan, Moscow to Mexico, and especially the United States the styles can be seen. Art Deco has a feeling of industry and luxury which made this a very popular style of business and commerce buildings. The phrase “Cathedral of Commerce” was coined to describe such structures with heavy influences on the interior and exterior design. There are even influences to the design and furnishing of ocean liners built during that time.
