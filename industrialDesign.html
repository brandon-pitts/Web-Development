Keeping with the themes of designing spaces that purvey a sense of luxury, Art Deco’s influence can be seen on industrial design.This style of design reaches into the 30s where everyday household items are built to model this style. From chairs and couches to lamps and vacuums Art Deco can seen while keeping with the feeling of high class. Pushing away from furniture while keeping in the same category as industrial design, you can even see Art Deco’s influence in cars and even locomotives of the time period.
